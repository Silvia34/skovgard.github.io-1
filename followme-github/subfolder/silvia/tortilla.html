Tortilla


WATCH this awesome video and try to make it at home!


Good Spanish food for lunch!

By Silvia Artero




Ingridients


  • 4 Eggs
  • 3 Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Salt

Tortilla is omelet in Spanish, don’t get confused by the tortilla of corn that you usually use to make burritos. The tortilla from Spain is made usually with eggs, potatoes and onions, there are some variants where you can add chorizo and red pepper.

It goes well with mayonnaise and other type of sauces.


If you want to learn more about how to make one and taste it watch the video and share the results with us in our Instagram!
